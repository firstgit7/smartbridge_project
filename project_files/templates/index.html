Tips for Applicants

Maximize your chances of an instant automated approval by following these guidelines before you apply:

Lower Your Debt Burden

Our AI factors in property and car ownership against your family size. Reducing other debts improves your financial stability score.

Employment Stability

Applicants with a longer tenure at their current occupation score significantly higher than frequent job-hoppers.

Accurate Income Reporting

Income per family member is a crucial metric. Ensure you report total household income accurately.

Bank Compliance Guidelines

For bank officers: How the AI enforces our strict risk-aversion protocols.

  • Strict 90% Threshold
    The Random Forest model requires an absolute probability >90% to trigger an auto-approval. This minimizes false positives (costly defaults).
  • SMOTE Balanced Training
    The model has been synthetically trained to heavily penalize missing risky applicants. It actively looks for high-risk flags in seemingly 'safe' applicants.
  • Manual Review Flags
    Applicants scoring between 85% and 90% are marked as 'Medium Risk' and can be intercepted for manual officer review on the dashboard.
